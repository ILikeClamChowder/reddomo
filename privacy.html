Privacy Policy

Reddomo keeps things simple. This page explains what information is (and isn't) collected when you visit and play.

The short version

You don't need an account to play — we don't ask for your name, email, or personal details. Your game high scores are saved only in your own browser (local storage), never sent to us.

Cookies & advertising

Reddomo is free and supported by ads. We use Google AdSense to display them. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to Reddomo and other sites.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can opt out of some third-party vendors' cookies at aboutads.info/choices.

Games from other sites

Some games on Reddomo are embedded from their official free websites (for example, Minecraft Classic and others). When you play those, you're interacting with that game's own site, which has its own privacy practices and may set its own cookies. We don't control those.

Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly, aggregate analytics to understand which games are popular. This data is anonymous and not used to identify you.

Children

Reddomo is a general-audience website and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page. Last updated July 2026.
