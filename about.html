Hi, I'm Domo.

Reddomo is a small, hand-built arcade of browser games. No installs, no accounts, no nonsense — click and play.

Quality over quantity

Most game sites throw hundreds of broken links at you. Not here. Every game is picked (or built) to actually work, load fast, and feel good — even on a school Chromebook.

Built to last

The games are self-hosted and open-source wherever possible, plus a few officially-free classics. That means they load quickly and don't vanish when some other website goes down.

More on the way

The shelf grows over time — originals you can't play anywhere else, alongside the classics you already love. Got one you'd like to see? Domo's listening.
